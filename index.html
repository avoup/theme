We predict your clients’ behaviour in various contexts

We use broad spectrum of machine learning techniques to perform cutting edge statistical analysis

Credit risk scoring: estimating client’s risk level using application or behavioral scorecards for effective underwriting

Propensity scoring (offer/campaign optimization): targeting clients with highest probability to buy a product thereby reducing costs of campaigns

Fraud alerts: designing intelligent algorithms for detecting fraudulent activities

Customer churn: identify customers that are most likely to leave. If a client is profitable it is essential to take an effort to retain her otherwise it is not worth to spend on keeping her engaged

What if analysis: simulating scenarios of business development given new decision engine and/or new business rules (assuming modifications made in decision engine and business rules are already in effect, implemented)

Segmentation: grouping customers based on the similarity of their characteristics helps us to identify most profitable market, or emerging market, to effectively plan crm activities, enables us to have segment based crm activities resulting in more effective campaigns

Pricing: estimating APR, etc

Actuarial research: evaluation of insurance risk in both health and non health sectors and product specific pricing
